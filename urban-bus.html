How to use the Urban Bus Services

All routes are complete loops. Once the bus departs the Main St Terminal (MST) it will travel in either a clockwise or anticlockwise direction until it arrives back at the Main St Terminal. Routes take approximately 35-40 minutes to complete.


  1. Find your current location on the map and where you wish to go.
  2. Choose the time you wish to travel and be at the stop 5 minutes prior.
  3. Please signal the driver when you see the bus approaching.
  4. On the bus, plcae your GoCard on the reader, or pay your cash fare and tell the driver your destination. Take your ticket with you, especially if you are transfering to another bus.
  5. Push the bell as you approach the stop.
